Introduction (CV)

Associate Professor, Harbin Engineering University

Tao received his B.S. in Automation and M.Eng in Software Engineering from Northeastern University. He obtained his Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from University of Seoul, under the supervision of Prof. Byungjeong Lee. After that, Tao became a Postdoctoral Research Fellow advised by Dr. Xiapu Luo.

His current research interests include Mining Software Repositories, Software Security, and Program Language.

I am looking for highly motivated Phd/MPhil students with strong background in Data Mining, Artificial Intelligence, Software Security, and Software Engineering. Undergraduate students who are interested in my research are also welcome to join us as research assistant.
